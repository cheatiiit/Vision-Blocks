What is Vision Blocks

VisionBlocks is here to solve your daily problems by presenting a simple drag-and-drop interface for you to build a computer vision program. No programming experience? No worries. VisionBlocks is exactly for you.

Can a grandma, with no prior programming experience, visit a website and build a computer vision application? Imagine a situation where you want to watch your baby when you are not in the room. Your friend wants to catch speeders in the neighborhood with his web-camera. Carol wants to track her favorite player in a football game. State-of-the-art computer vision algorithms can easily provide solutions in such situations. Via VisionBlocks, these can all be easily built and customized for your usage.

Are you a talented computer vision wizard want to show off your skill? VisionBlocks is the best place. Create a block and help thousands of people to solve their problems. With great power comes great responsibility.
